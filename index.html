Quiz Competition Rules

1. Eligibility:

  1. Participants must register prior to the competition.
  2. Each participant must provide valid identification and proof of eligibility.
  3. The competition is open to individuals as specified in the competition announcement.

2. Format:

  1. The quiz will consist of multiple rounds including preliminary, semi-final, and final rounds.
  2. Questions will cover various topics as outlined in the competition syllabus.
  3. Each round will have a set number of questions.

3. Questions:

  1. Questions will be multiple-choice, true/false, or short answer format.
  2. Each question will be displayed on the screen for a specific amount of time.
  3. No points will be awarded for unanswered questions.
How to Play:
  1. Choose a Quiz: Browse our collection of quizzes or select a category that interests you..
  2. Answer Questions: Answer each question to the best of your ability. Don't worry if you get stuck; just give it your best shot!.
  3. Beat the Clock: Some quizzes have a time limit, so keep an eye on the timer and try to answer quickly.
  4. View Results: After completing a quiz, see how you did and challenge yourself to improve your score.
